Sports & Game Watching Events

Our chapter hosts game watching events for all ASU football games. Our home bar is the Crystal City Sports Pub in Pentagon City. During all ASU games, we have drink specials.

We're not just into watching -- our chapter likes to play social sports as well! We're a proud member of the Capital Alumni Network, and we participate in the softball league every summer.

To read more about ASU's local softball team, go to our softball page.
