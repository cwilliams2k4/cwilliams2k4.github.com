Our alumni chapter participates in the Capital Alumni Network's softball league.

Each year, the Arizona State softball team plays a 14-game schedule. Most games are played on the national mall, though some weekend games are held at the Goddard Softball Fields in Laurel, Maryland.

Every August, CAN hosts a tournament for all participating teams. The whole experience is a lot of fun! In 2015, the softball tournament will take place August 8th, 9th, and 15th.

Please email our coaches, Jon Lyons and Blake Holub, if you have any further questions!
