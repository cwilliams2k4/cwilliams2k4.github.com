Pac-12 D.C. Alumni Happy Hour

Thursday, March 12, 5:30 p.m.
Penn Social

Join the ASU National Capital Chapter and other Pac-12 D.C. alumni for our annual Pac-12 Men's Basketball Tournament Happy Hour. We are holding it at Penn Social this year and our games will be on the big screen, starting at 5:30 p.m.! Doors open at 4 p.m.

ASU Cares Volunteer Project

Saturday, April 4, 11 a.m. – 2 p.m.
Homeless Children's Playtime Project Office
St. Stephens Church
1525 Newton St. NW
Washington, DC

Volunteers will assist the Homeless Children's Playtime Project prepare for our Annual Spring Backpack Distribution. These backpacks are filled with school supplies and activity kits. We provide school supplies in the spring to help ensure children have enough supplies for the last portion of the school year.

Our group will be stuffing, sorting and boxing backpacks by shelter site and age.

Pat's Run Shadow Run

Saturday, April 25

Through a partnership with the ASU Alumni Association, you can Run/Walk and raise money to support Tillman Military Scholars, the same as you would in Tempe.

Shadow Runs will bolster our awareness and fundraising efforts. So if you can’t make the big Tempe event, we’d love for you to participate in the Shadow Run hosted by the ASU Alumni Association's National Capital Chapter, and join others who are passionate about our mission at this casual and fun run.
